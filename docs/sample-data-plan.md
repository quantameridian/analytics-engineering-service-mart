# Sample Data Plan

## Planned seed files

- `seeds/raw_cases.csv`
- `seeds/raw_teams.csv`
- `seeds/raw_service_events.csv`
- `seeds/raw_case_categories.csv`
- `seeds/raw_targets.csv`

## Data scenarios

- cases opened and closed across several teams
- events showing assignment, review, pause, and closure
- team reference data with active and inactive teams
- SLA eligible and non-eligible records
- reopened, paused, cancelled, overdue, and late-closed examples

The sample data must remain synthetic and free from client, protected, official, or internal workplace material.

## Current status

The seed files have been created. See `docs/data-dictionary.md` for field definitions, row counts, assumptions, and intentional modelling imperfections.
